ABOUT US

Department of Civil Engineering is the first department to be set up at NIE. Right from the inception the department is providing high quality value based education to the students. The department has made focussed effort in carrying out research activities in the field of Structural engineering related to Masonry, Fire resistance,Fibre reinforced concrete,Structural reliability, Earthquake Engineering Ferro cement and also in the Stream of water resources. The flagship event of the department is AAKAR, a national level technical extravaganza.

ABOUT AAKAR 2016

AAKAR is a biennial National Level Technical Festival of Civil Engineering department held once in two years. Since Its inception in 2006, AAKAR has grown to become one of the largest civil engineering fests of the state.AAKAR 2016 is the sixth in the series. This year AAKAR is going international by conducting international symposium on infrastructure and heritage structures. Eminent speakers from India and Abroad will be delivering lectures in this symposium and Musical Night by Bansuri Maestro Pravin Godkhindi .
