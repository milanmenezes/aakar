ACCOMMODATION

NIE has always been well-known for its warm hospitality. The friendly atmosphere among students and the pleasant environment here offer more to its sojourners than just accommodation. As a participant of the Fest, your hospitality is our primary concern. The accommodation will be at Youth Hostel with good facilities and is free of cost .

We will make sure that your short stay with us will be pleasant and comfortable. The Fest will surely be a memorable experience for you. To assist you in the best possible manner, we require a few preliminary things to be checked from your side. First, you must make sure that you register yourself in our website through our online registration feature. Kindly register yourself as soon as the registration lines open. A mail will be sent to you confirming your registration. Once registered, you can interact with us through the email to enquire further regarding the fest.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:
  • All student participants must carry their valid College photo ID card.
  • People without valid photo identity card will not be allowed inside the campus during Aakar.
  • All students need to report at Registration Desk on arri-val at NIE Campus.
  • Accomodation for first come first served.
  • A team of six participants from each college is eligible to participate.
  • Registered participants should confirm their participation 15 days before fest.
  • Participants have to take care of their belongings.
  • The organising team won’t be responsible if anything is lost.
  • Students are solely responsible for any damage to the infrastructure in the room and in case of any such inci-dents, management decision will be final.
  • Random checks would be made to avoid any illegal stay at the campus.
  • Any team failing to produce their receipts of accommodation would be fined and disqualified.
  • However, you are encouraged to carry your own blankets since it might be a little cold at night.
  • At the time of check-out the participants are required to return all the commodities provided to them to the or-ganisers. Heavy penalties will be imposed by the organ-ising team if anything is missing. The incident would be reported to respective college authorities.

REACHING NIE,Mysuru The modes of travelling in Mysuru are Ola,Uber and Meru cabs , auto rickshaws,Jugnoo autos, City Buses(KSRTC). NIE is located at Manandvadi Road.

  • Those who are coming by Train can make use of prepaid auto service.
  • Those who are coming by bus can make use of prepaid au-to service.
